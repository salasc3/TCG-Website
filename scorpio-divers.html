About Scorpio Divers

Where serious
divers train

Scorpio Divers is The Chilam Group's dedicated technical training center. Focused on cave, cavern, and advanced open-water specialties, we work exclusively with divers who want to develop real technical competence — not just collect certification cards.

Our instructors have thousands of cave dives each, know the Yucatan cave system intimately, and are certified to teach across PADI and GUE training frameworks. This is where professional and expedition divers come to prepare for the hardest dives of their careers.

Training Programs

From cavern
to full cave

Level 1

Entry

Cavern Diver

The first step into overhead environments. Cavern diving within the daylight zone, guided by certified cave divers. Open to all certified OW divers.

Level 2

Intermediate

Intro to Cave

Penetration diving beyond natural light using guideline and lights. Single tank configurations, limited penetration distance.

Level 3

Advanced

Full Cave Diver

Full cave diving certification — the definitive qualification for unrestricted cave exploration. Two weeks of intensive training in Yucatan cave systems.

Specialty

Technical

Sidemount & Backmount Cave

Configuration-specific cave certifications. Learn to manage doubles, sidemount, and stage cylinders in confined overhead environments. Essential for serious cave explorers.

Elite

Trimix

Deep Cave & Trimix

For experienced cave divers pushing into deeper systems. Trimix certification combined with deep cave protocols — preparation for expedition-level diving. By application only.
