About Scorpio

Cancun's
diving gateway

Founded in 2012 and acquired by The Chilam Group in 2024, Scorpio Divers operates in Cancun as both a high-volume recreational dive center and a strategic feeder for the NUCLEO network. The operation serves one of the world's busiest coastal tourism hubs.

Scorpio introduces hundreds of new divers to the region each year through reef dives, discover scuba programs, certifications, and seasonal whale shark trips. It functions as the commercial bridge between mainstream Cancun tourism and the specialized diving corridors deeper inside the Yucatan Peninsula.
