XTC Dive Center

TCG's flagship marine operation in Xcalak, Quintana Roo. Reef and offshore diving, fly fishing, boutique lodging, and guided adventures at the edge of Banco Chinchorro Biosphere Reserve.

About XTC

Ocean diving,
Xcalak

XTC Dive Center is TCG's flagship operation in Xcalak, the southernmost town in Quintana Roo. The site integrates reef diving, offshore expeditions, fly fishing, boutique lodging, and an oceanfront restaurant under one management structure within the Xcalak Reef National Park.

Located 36 nautical miles from the Banco Chinchorro Biosphere Reserve, XTC provides access to some of the least-visited reef systems in the Caribbean. Every program is led by certified, English-speaking guides with deep local knowledge of Xcalak's marine environment.

What We Offer

Programs & Experiences

Recreational

Reef Snorkeling

No experience needed. Xcalak's protected reef is among the most pristine in the Caribbean. Guided snorkel tours daily.

Introductory

Discover Scuba

First-time dives on the Xcalak reef. Controlled, shallow reef environments with expert supervision. PADI-certified program.

Advanced

Reef & Wall Diving

For certified divers. Xcalak's reef wall drops into deep Caribbean blue. Guided dives on pristine coral systems with exceptional visibility.

Technical

Offshore & Banco Chinchorro

Multi-day offshore expeditions to the Banco Chinchorro Biosphere Reserve. Crocodile encounters, wreck dives, and remote reef systems accessible only by boat from Xcalak.

Specialty

Fly Fishing & Adventure

XTC operates guided fly fishing on the Xcalak flats. The area is world-renowned for permit, bonefish, and tarpon. Private guides, full equipment, half and full-day formats.
