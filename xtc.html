XTC Dive Center

The Riviera Maya's premier cenote and cavern dive center. The world's most spectacular underwater landscapes become accessible to explorers at every level.

About XTC

Cenote diving,
done right

XTC Dive Center has been guiding divers through the Yucatan's legendary underwater cave systems since the early 2000s. Our guides are among the most experienced cave divers in the world, with thousands of cave dives each and an uncompromising commitment to safety and guest experience.

Whether you're taking your first breath underwater or pushing into unexplored cavern passages, XTC has a program for you. Every dive is led by certified, English-speaking guides who know these cenotes the way others know their own backyards.
