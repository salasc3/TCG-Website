Operations & Investment

The Architecture of Performance. TCG delivers the full operating and investment layer for high-friction hospitality assets. Our services are structured as specific interventions or full vertical management, depending on the maturity and needs of the project.

The Structural Problem

The Operator's Dilemma

In remote, high-friction environments, hospitality assets fail due to structural collapse, not market failure. The harder a single operator works to scale, the more the administrative foundation fractures. Finance becomes manual. Compliance falls behind. Margins erode before the asset reaches its revenue potential.

TCG solves this through the NUCLEO framework. We decouple administration, finance, HR, and marketing from the physical experience site. On-site operators focus on service delivery, safety, and hospitality. We manage the rest.

What We Offer

Our services

Each service removes a specific category of structural risk. They operate independently or as a unified mandate, depending on the asset.

01

Investment as a Service

TCG structures the financial and legal foundation of the asset before capital enters. We develop financial projections that reflect site-specific constraints, build entity structures designed to isolate risk and ensure cross-border compliance, and prepare the asset for institutional-grade reporting. Our role centers on readiness and alignment. Capital enters a structure that is legally sound, financially transparent, and operationally viable.

Financial Modeling Capital Strategy Legal Architecture Cross-Border Compliance Incubation
02

Operations as a Service

TCG assumes full responsibility for on-site and off-site operations. On the ground, our teams are structured to perform in demanding environments without compromising service quality or safety standards. Off-site, we centralize HR, accounting, legal, PR, and executive oversight into a shared infrastructure. Every managed site draws from this central engine. Administration costs drop. Execution quality rises.

On-Site Management Centralized Back-Office Safety Systems HR & Staffing PROTEUS Stack
03

Distribution & Tour Operation

Managed brands gain immediate access to TCG's established relationships with major outbound tour operators. We operate a shared commercial backend across all portfolio brands. Sales channels are unified, leads are tracked, and every booking is professionally handled. By managing multiple experience brands within the same network, TCG creates a cross-selling flywheel that drives incremental revenue for every asset in the portfolio without increasing the cost of acquisition.

Wholesale Network Inbound Sales Cross-Selling Booking Infrastructure

Experience & White-Label Programs
