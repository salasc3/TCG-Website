Framework

The NUCLEO
Model

NUCLEO is TCG's operational framework for building interlocking ventures. Each entity shares infrastructure, staff, and logistics with the others. The result is a network where each venture's performance directly supports the group's overall margin and commercial position.

The model eliminates the redundant overhead that degrades single-venue operators. One fleet, one ops team, one booking system, one safety standard. Four businesses. Compounding returns.

N — Natural assets Built on location advantages with structural barriers to replication
U — Unique Product experiences that cannot be reproduced at scale
C — Cultural context Operated within the legal, regulatory, and social environment of the Yucatan
L — Low-volume Small-group formats that protect margin, safety, and product quality
E — Economy Commercially viable at every stage of the asset lifecycle
O — Off-the-beaten-path Locations with structural protection from mass-market competition
