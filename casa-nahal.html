About The Dark Zone

Cenote diving,
done right

The Dark Zone is TCG's Xpu Ha operation, a reimagined multi-venture site where cave and cavern diving, advanced training, and creative hospitality converge. Located between Playa del Carmen and Akumal, the site provides access to the Yucatan's most significant cenote systems.

The site runs three distinct operations: recreational cenote diving and PADI courses, advanced cave diving and scientific collaboration through Dark Zone Diving, and rebreather and CCR training through the Rebreather Academy. The Bat House Café supports staff and guests on-site.
